Halloween

Halloween is celebrated on October 31st.

Some people have Halloween parties. Some people like to carve a pumpkin into aJack-O-Lantern.

Some people take their kids trick-or-treating to get candy. Some other people play tricks.

Be Safe When Trick-or-Treating

Tips for being safe when you are trick-or-treating:

  • Wear reflective clothing
  • Stay in a group. Don't go out alone
  • Only go to houses with lights on
  • Carry a flashlight
  • Don't wear a mask that makes it difficult to see or breath
  • Stay hydrated
  • Look for Trunk-or-Treat events. This is a great way to stock up on som goodies in a short amount of time and is alot of times safer than going to a neighborhood you aren't familiar with.

Following these tips can help keep you safe during your trick-or-trating adventure.
